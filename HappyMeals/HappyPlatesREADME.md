## About

HappyPlates is a new-age hospitality venture based in Bengaluru, India. Since its inception in 2022, it has grown into a trusted catering brand serving corporate clients, event organizers, and private clients across the city.

Their philosophy: *"From timeless classics to contemporary global fusions, each offering reflects a blend of culinary artistry, cultural richness, and seasonal freshness."*
